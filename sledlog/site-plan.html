Overview

Purpose

The Snowmobile Maintenance Log helps snowmobile owners track service tasks, part replacements, and ride logs to keep their machines in peak condition.

Audience

This site is designed for snowmobile owners and hobbyists who prefer to maintain their own machines and want an easy way to stay organized.

Dynamic elements

This site will use JavaScript to Add, display, and delete maintenance log entries, filter logs by service type (e.g., oil change, track inspection, clutch work), highlight overdue maintenance based on date, use objects and arrays to store service data, and utilize forEach() and filter() methods, react to form submissions and button clicks to dynamically update the DOM.
